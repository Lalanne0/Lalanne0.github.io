About me

My name is Vincent Lalanne and I am a student at the École des Mines de Saint-Étienne, a prestigious French engineering school.

I have always wanted to get involved in state-of-the-art technology, that is why I chose to specialise in computer science and microelectronics. I believe that knowing how a computer works at the hardware level allows a deeper thinking and better efficiency on the code I write.

In the future, I want to bring together my skills and my passions: valuating data by creating innovative and inclusive AI models, improving people's daily and working lives, or helping research on various subjects.

I feel motivated by knowing that my contribution will be useful to other people, and am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow.

I am currently looking for building up experience, and discovering new work environments. Check out my CV here!
