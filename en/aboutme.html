About me

My name is Vincent Lalanne, I am a student at the École des Mines de Saint-Étienne, a top French engineering school. I studied maths, physics and engineering in detail for 2 years in a preparatory class to integrate it, and still love these subjects!

I have always wanted to get involved in state-of-the-art technology, that is why I chose to specialise in computer science and microelectronics. I feel motivated by knowing that my contribution will be useful to other people, and am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow.

I remain curious about all sorts of things, because I believe that understanding the work of others plays a major role in the success of our own.

In the future, I want to bring together my skills and my passions: understand a software's architecture, secure a web application, create websites, coding a modern AI...

I am currently looking forward to buiding up experience, and discovering work environments abroad. Check out my CV here!
